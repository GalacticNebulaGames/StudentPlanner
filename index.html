The problem: You don't know what to do next, so you open YouTube instead. This guide gives you a clear start point for every session — no thinking required.
// SESSION STRUCTURE
01
SESSION START RITUAL
Do this before EVERY session — 5 mins
5 MIN▼
  • 01Open Upskilled portal. Find exactly where you left off.
  • 02Write down ONE goal for this session on paper or Notepad. One task, not a list.
  • 03Phone: Do Not Disturb ON. Put it face-down across the room.
  • 04Close every browser tab except the course. No "I'll just check one thing."
  • 05Start a timer (25 min Pomodoro). Begin.
02
POMODORO CYCLE (CORE METHOD)
25 min work → 5 min break, repeat
LOOP▼
  • →25 min: Work on ONE thing. Don't switch. If stuck, write the problem down and keep going or skip it.
  • →5 min: Stand up, stretch, water. No phone, no YouTube. Hard rule.
  • →After 4 cycles (2 hrs), take a 20–30 min real break. Food, outside, whatever.
  • 💡If you get into flow and don't want to stop at 25 min — don't. Ride the flow. Only use breaks when you feel resistance.
03
WHEN YOU GET STUCK ON A BUG
Don't let one bug eat a whole session
PROTOCOL▼
  • 01Spend max 15 min on it solo. Actually read the error message first.
  • 02Google the exact error message. Check Stack Overflow or docs.
  • 03Still stuck? Ask Claude, your trainer, or course forums. Don't grind alone.
  • 04If it's completely blocking you — move to the next task and come back. Don't let one bug kill your momentum.
  • ⚠Going down a rabbit hole trying to understand everything is a trap. Learn enough to move forward, deep dive later.
04
SESSION CLOSE RITUAL
End every session intentionally — 5 mins
5 MIN▼
  • 01Write down where you left off — exact page, task, or line of code. This is your start point next session. No hunting.
  • 02Write ONE thing you learned today. Even if it's small. Reinforces memory.
  • 03Close the course portal. You're done. Don't guilt-browse.
  • 04Go enjoy your earned free time without thinking about study.
05
3-WEEK RAMP PLAN
Don't try to go 0 → 5 hrs overnight
RAMP▼
  • WK12 hrs/day target. Two sessions, 1 hr each. Build the habit first.
  • WK23.5 hrs/day target. Add the second session properly + one night block.
  • WK35 hrs/day target. Full schedule. All three sessions running.
  • 💡Consistency over volume. 3 hrs every day beats 8 hrs once a week and nothing for 4 days.
YouTube and gaming aren't the enemy — unearned YouTube and gaming are. These rules make free time guilt-free by making it conditional.
// ANTI-DISTRACTION RULES
PHONE EXILE
During any study session, phone goes face-down across the room. Not next to you. Across the room. Notification buzz = invisible.
NO TAB ROULETTE
One tab open: the course. If you need to Google something, do it, then close the tab. No "while I'm here" browsing.
GAMES AFTER SESSIONS
Zero gaming before your daily session minimum is done. After it's done, full permission, no guilt. This makes gaming a reward, not an escape.
NO "JUST ONE VIDEO"
YouTube during study breaks is a trap. 5 min becomes 45. Use breaks to stand up and move instead. YouTube is post-session only.
MUSIC IS ALLOWED
Lo-fi, instrumental, game OSTs, anything without lyrics. Queue it up before you start so you don't need to touch YouTube mid-session.
WRITE IT DOWN
Every new concept you figure out — write it in your own words. This is your strongest memory tool and it's backed by how your brain actually works.
SKIP AND RETURN
Stuck on something? Note it, skip it, keep moving. Progress feels better than perfect. You can always come back.
WEEKLY REVIEW (SUNDAY)
10 mins Sunday: what did you cover this week? What's next? This prevents Monday "where was I" paralysis — your biggest time killer.
Use this as your daily pre-flight. Check things off as you go. Resets each day when you reload.
